Don't Miss Out: Newark Public Art Map

Be sure to check out the recently launched Newark public art map! This is the largest collection of public art in the city of Newark, featuring over 100 murals + installations throughout the city along with profiles of the amazing artists behind them! This mural map is a visual documentation of the art culture that drives the city of Newark, past and present and is Newark Arts' gift to the city in honor of our 40th anniversary.

Sponsored by the Greater Newark Convention and Visitors Bureau and in collaboration with the City of Newark, Four Corners Public Arts, and multiple artists and collaborators across the city, the mobile-friendly website maps out the citys murals and public art projects created by local and international artists.

Countdown to Newark Arts Festival 2021!

The countdown begins to Newark Arts Festival 2021 ("NAF") will celebrate Creative Resilience, as it returns to in-person programming! NAF amplifies the voices of established and emerging Newark artists, gallerists, and creative entrepreneurs.

Be sure to sign up to stay up to date on the art festival Essence just named in their Top 15 Festivals to Pull Up To This Year at newarkartsfestival.com. About Newark Arts

Newark Arts mission is to power the arts to transform lives. Established in 1981 as the Newark Arts Council, the organizations vision is to become a nationally recognized catalyst for the collaborative power of the arts in the City of Newark and urban America. Newark Arts is the producer of Newark Creates, a community-led citywide cultural plan, created in partnership with the City of Newark. Newark Arts key initiatives include Arts Education Newark (AEN), the ArtStart community grantmaking program, and the Newark Arts Festival. Newark Arts is generously supported by Prudential Financial, the City of Newark, M&T Bank, PSEG, Geraldine R. Dodge Foundation, Victoria Foundation, Turrell Fund, Bank of America, New Jersey State Council on the Arts, the National Endowment for the Arts, Verizon, United Airlines, Hanini Group and individuals, businesses and institutions.

To keep up-to-date on all the latest news from Newark Arts, sign up for our newsletter at newarkarts.org and follow @Newark_Arts on Twitter and @Newark_Arts on Facebook and Instagram.

Newark Arts was created to advance and expand the artistic and cultural resources of New Jersey's largest city. Our mission is to bring the transformative power of the arts into the lives of those who live in, work in, and visit Newark through programs, advocacy, promotion, education, and coordination.
